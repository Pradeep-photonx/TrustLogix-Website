The Challenge

Posture tools find everything—and tell you what matters nowhere

As data spreads across the lakehouse, entitlements pile up, policies drift, and every scanner hands you thousands of context-free alerts. Without knowing which data is sensitive to your business—or who actually needs it—teams can't tell a real exposure from noise. Risk grows, audits drag, and least privilege stays a slide, not a reality.

What TrustDSPM Does

Discover risk, right-size access, prove compliance—continuously

One control plane that understands the business context of your data, so every recommendation is one you can actually act on.

Continuous Risk Discovery

  • Sensitive data, classified in context

    Auto-discover and tag PII, financial and regulated data by business meaning.

  • Over-privileged access exposed

    See who can reach what—and how—across every platform.

  • Posture scored, not just listed

    A prioritized score that ranks exposures by real business impact.

Least-Privilege Recommendations

  • Right-size entitlements automatically

    Get specific, ready-to-apply policy fixes—not vague advice.

  • Row- & column-level masking

    Recommend fine-grained masking and filtering where it's needed.

  • Stop policy drift before it starts

    Stay aligned as users, data and platforms keep changing.

Compliance, Made Continuous

  • Audit-ready evidence on demand

    Living proof for SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA and NIST—no fire drills.

  • Map controls to frameworks

    See exactly which policies satisfy which requirements.

  • Feeds your SIEM

    Stream posture signals to Splunk, Sentinel and Chronicle.
