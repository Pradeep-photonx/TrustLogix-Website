FAQ

Pricing FAQs

Discover the answers to your most pressing questions about TrustLogix pricing.

Get StartedGet Started
What is included in the Departmental plan?
The Departmental plan includes access to one Snowflake or Databricks warehouse of any size, the complete TrustLogix Platform with TrustDSPM, TrustAccess, TrustAI modules, plus 24/7 support with Rapid Response SLA. It's designed for teams that want enterprise-grade security without the enterprise sprawl, friction, or operational overhead.
How quickly can we activate the platform?
You can activate TrustLogix in 1–2 hours. Our metadata-only, proxyless architecture makes setup fast and seamless.
Can we deploy TrustLogix in our cloud?
Yes, TrustLogix deploys directly in your cloud, wherever your data lives.
Does TrustLogix store or see my data?
No, TrustLogix is metadata-only. So TrustLogix doesn't store or see your data.
Do you support on-premise platforms?
Yes, TrustLogix fully supports on-premises deployments. The platforms include Oracle Database, MySQL, Microsoft SQL Server and PostgreSQL.
Do you charge per user / do you charge by amount of data used?
No, we do not charge per user or by the amount of data you use. Our pricing is not tied to user count, data volume, or query usage. This ensures predictable costs as your data estate and teams grow.
What is the cost for non-production vs production environments?
Non-production environments can be provisioned at a discounted rate (typically 50% of production), allowing teams to test and validate configurations before deployment.
Do you offer multi-year discounts?
Yes, we offer multi-year discounts. Please contact our sales team for details.
How does pricing scale across clouds?
Pricing scales consistently across clouds. TrustLogix uses a unified pricing model, so Snowflake, Databricks, and all cloud platforms are billed the same.
Can we purchase TrustLogix through a cloud marketplace?
Yes. TrustLogix is available through AWS Marketplace and Snowflake Marketplace, making procurement faster and allowing you to leverage existing cloud spend commitments.
Do you support large Enterprise deployments?
Yes. TrustLogix already supports some of the world's largest Fortune 100 enterprises — securing tens of petabytes of data, governing millions of automated permissions, and protecting 350K+ employees across multi-cloud and on-prem environments.
