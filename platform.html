Architecture

Enterprise-Grade Security Architecture

Every layer of the TrustLogix platform is designed for enterprise-scale security, data residency, and operational control.

Flexible Deployment

Choose a fully hosted TrustLogix deployment or run the data plane in your own cloud. Full control over where your data processing happens.

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

Encrypt all metadata with your own keys to maintain complete security ownership and satisfy the strictest data sovereignty requirements.

Private Connectivity

Use private links for all API calls between TrustLogix and your data platforms — ensuring traffic never traverses the public internet.

Controlled Access

Service accounts connect via OAuth client secrets stored in a secure secrets manager, with IP restrictions for added protection.

Global Availability

Deployed across US and EU regions to meet data residency and compliance requirements wherever your business operates.

Tenant Isolation by Design

Serverless compute always runs within the customer's network boundary to enforce strong isolation between tenants.

FAQ

Platform Module FAQs

Everything you need to know about the TrustLogix platform, modules, and getting started.

What is TrustLogix?
TrustLogix is a patented, AI-powered data security platform that unifies DSPM, access governance, and AI data protection in one control plane. It continuously detects excessive permissions, right-sizes entitlements, and enforces least-privilege policies across platforms like Snowflake, Databricks, Power BI, and AI agents.
How does it work?
TrustLogix delivers unified, AI-powered data security through consistent, least-privilege enforcement across cloud, analytics, and AI environments. It provides continuous visibility into which data is most critical, who's accessing it, and where risks exist — using AI-driven insights to recommend remediations and refine policies. With automated, audit-ready compliance reporting and native, no-proxy integration, TrustLogix activates in minutes to hours.
Who can benefit?
  • Modern data enterprises with multi-platform analytics, AI, and BI stacks gain unified governance and consistent security across Snowflake, Databricks, and Power BI.
  • Regulated industries such as healthcare, finance, and telecom protect PHI and PII with fine-grained, compliant access controls.
  • Security and data leaders — CISOs, CDOs, and their teams — get visibility, accountability, and control in a single platform.
  • Modernization programs migrating to the cloud, ensuring seamless policy continuity across legacy, hybrid, and cloud environments.
What are the features?
TrustLogix delivers AI-powered data security through three integrated modules: TrustDSPM provides continuous visibility into sensitive data and access risks. TrustAccess enforces fine-grained, least-privilege policies across platforms with intuitive no-code controls. TrustAI secures AI and agent workflows by applying policy-based access and monitoring data interactions. Together, they deliver unified visibility, control, and protection.
How do I get started?
Getting started is easy. Simply contact us to discuss your challenges with a data security expert and explore our platform. Our team will guide you through setup and answer any questions you have. Most customers are up and running within minutes.
