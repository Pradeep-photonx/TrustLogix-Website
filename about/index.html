TrustLogix is the AI-powered platform for unified data control. It keeps access, protection, and compliance in sync across all clouds and data platforms—helping enterprises move faster and innovate safely.

About TrustLogix

Securing the Data That Drives AI

Our Mission

Purpose-built to secure the data that drives AI.

As enterprises share data across clouds and build Gen-AI applications, they face unprecedented security and compliance challenges. TrustLogix was founded to meet them head-on — a no-proxy, high-performance platform that unifies policy-based access controls and data security posture management, so teams can confidently govern data across every cloud and on-premise environment.

Our Foundation

Shaped by two decades in security.

Led by veterans of Cloud Security, Data Security, and Identity Management, our team has architected platforms that protect hundreds of thousands of organizations — powering enterprise apps, cloud services, and identity systems across industries and continents.

Our Heritage

Pioneers of API-first cloud security.

Our journey began with Palerra, the industry's first API-based CASB, which redefined how enterprises secure the cloud. Since then we've partnered with global organizations on their toughest data security, privacy, and compliance challenges.
