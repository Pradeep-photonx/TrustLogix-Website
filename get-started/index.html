How does the startup process work?
Activate TrustLogix in minutes — no proxies, agents, or changes to your existing data stack. You'll see data sprawl and activity dashboards the same day you start, and full role- and attribute-based access controls within hours.
What's included?
TrustDSPM, TrustAccess, and TrustAI — the full unified data control platform, with activation and implementation assistance from our team.
Which data platforms are supported?
Snowflake, Databricks, Power BI, AWS Redshift, BigQuery, SQL Server, and more — TrustLogix runs across the modern data stack without requiring agents or proxies in your data path.
Is training and support available?
Yes — email support on the POC, and 24/7 support on paid plans. Our customer success team partners with you through onboarding, rollout, and expansion.
